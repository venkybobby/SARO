## Important Disclaimer

SARO is an **AI risk assessment and audit tool**. It is not a certification body, legal advisor, or regulatory authority. The findings and risk scores produced by SARO are analytical aids — they do not constitute legal compliance, regulatory approval, or certification.

## Framework Coverage Matrix

### NIST AI Risk Management Framework (RMF 1.0)

| SARO Supports | SARO Does NOT Replace |
|--------------|----------------------|
| Automated risk scoring against NIST RMF categories (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage) | Formal NIST AI RMF organizational programme implementation |
| Rule-based flagging of AI outputs against RMF principles | Human judgement in RMF governance decisions |
| Evidence export with NIST RMF citations | Attestation or certification by a NIST-recognized body |
| 90-day trend tracking of AI risk scores | Ongoing human oversight of AI systems |
| TRACE explanations of which RMF controls were evaluated | Legal counsel review of compliance posture |

---

### EU AI Act (2024/1689)

| SARO Supports | SARO Does NOT Replace |
|--------------|----------------------|
| Automated flagging of AI outputs against EU AI Act high-risk categories | Conformity assessments as defined in Article 43 |
| Risk classification assistance (Annex III risk categories) | Certification or CE marking — SARO does not issue certifications |
| Evidence documentation for internal compliance reviews | Registration in the EU AI Act database (Article 49) |
| Audit trail for AI system outputs used in regulated contexts | Notified body assessment for high-risk AI systems |
| TRACE explanations for non-technical reviewers | Legal determination of whether a system is "high-risk" under Article 6 |

**EXPLICIT DISCLAIMER:** SARO does not perform conformity assessments as defined in the EU AI Act. SARO does not issue CE markings, declarations of conformity, or any certification recognised under the EU AI Act.

## What SARO Does in Every Audit

1. **Ingests** AI model outputs for analysis
2. **Classifies** outputs against 4 gates: data quality, fairness, MIT risk, compliance
3. **Scores** risk using Bayesian posterior estimation
4. **Explains** findings in natural language with rule citations
5. **Records** a cryptographically signed audit trail for every analysis
6. **Exports** regulator-ready evidence packs (PDF + signed JSON)

## What SARO Never Does

- Issues compliance certificates or legal opinions
- Replaces human oversight of AI systems
- Makes binding regulatory determinations
- Stores or trains on your data beyond the audit session
- Guarantees that a passing score means regulatory approval
