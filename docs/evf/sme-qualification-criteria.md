# SARO External SME Validation Framework — SME Qualification Criteria

> Implements **FR-EVF-01**. Defines the minimum bar an external Subject-Matter Expert (SME)
> firm or individual must meet before being shortlisted to issue a Qualified Compliance
> Opinion (QCO) for any SARO framework scope. This document is internal governance material;
> it does not itself constitute a certification, conformity assessment, or legal advice.

## 1. Independence Requirements

- The SME must have **no financial interest** in SARO, its parent, or its investors, and no
  contingent-fee arrangement tied to the outcome of the opinion.
- The SME must not have participated in building, marketing, or selling SARO.
- A signed Conflict-of-Interest declaration (FR-EVF-03, `coi-declaration-form`) is mandatory
  **before** any Evidence Package is shared.

## 2. Credential Requirements (per framework)

| Framework | Minimum credential evidence |
|---|---|
| EU AI Act | Demonstrated regulatory practice in EU AI Act / Regulation (EU) 2024/1689; counsel or accredited assessor. |
| NIST AI RMF 1.0 | Documented experience applying the NIST AI RMF to production AI systems. |
| AIGP | IAPP AIGP certification or equivalent governance credential. |
| ISO 42001 | ISO/IEC 42001 lead-auditor or management-system assessment experience. |

Each shortlisted firm must supply at least **two named individuals** per framework with
verifiable credentials and references.

## 3. Sector and Methodology Experience

- Prior engagements auditing or assessing third-party AI systems in a regulated sector.
- Familiarity with evidence-based (non-certifying) assurance models.
- Ability to render a written professional opinion structured per FR-EVF-09 (QCO template).

## 4. Disqualifying Factors

- Any undisclosed conflict of interest discovered at any stage (immediate disqualification).
- Inability or unwillingness to retain editorial independence over opinion content.
- History of issuing certifications that misrepresent evidence-only assurance as conformity.

## 5. Selection Record

For each engagement, the Product Owner records: candidate firms considered, credentials
verified, COI status, and the selection rationale. Two COI-cleared candidates per framework
must be on file before a Statement of Work (FR-EVF-04) is executed.

---

*Scope boundary: A qualified SME issues a professional opinion (QCO) for internal SARO
governance. Nothing in this document authorises any party to certify SARO or to assert
regulatory conformity on SARO's behalf.*
